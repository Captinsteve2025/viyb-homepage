## üéØ What It Does

The calculator helps potential yacht charter owners estimate:

- **Purchase financing** - Loan payments, down payment, interest
- **Charter revenue** - Weekly rates, utilization, management fees
- **Annual expenses** - Maintenance, insurance, other costs
- **ROI metrics** - Net income, cash flow, payback period
- **Utilization** - Charter weeks vs personal use vs downtime

### Live Calculations

All calculations update in real-time as users adjust sliders:
- Purchase price ($500K - $3M)
- Down payment (20% - 50%)
- Charter weeks (10-35 per year)
- Weekly rates ($15K - $100K)
- Personal use (2-12 weeks)
- All expense inputs
